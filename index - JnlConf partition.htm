Biography

I am a Scientist and Principal Investigator at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), A*STAR. I received my PhD degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2009. I was a postdoctoral research fellow of the Computer Networks & Distributed Systems Lab (CNDS) at NUS from late 2008 to early 2010, and a postdoctoral research fellow of the Communications & Networks Lab (CNL) at NUS from 2011 to March 2012. I worked in industry of Singapore in 2010 and in China Telecom earlier on.

Research

  • My research interests are crowdsourcing, participatory sensing, (incentive) mechanism design, and network economics.
  • My earlier research was in the areas of (1) wireless networks (ad hoc, sensor, mesh, cognitive radio), and (2) software-defined networking (OpenFlow).
